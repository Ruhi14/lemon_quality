Good quality Lemon

High-quality lemons exhibit several key characteristics that signify their excellence. A vibrant, uniform yellow color without any green patches or discolorations is essential, and the skin should be smooth, free from blemishes, scars, or wrinkles.

Bad quality Lemon

Low-quality lemons exhibit several distinct characteristics that make them easily identifiable. They often have uneven coloration, with patches of green or brown indicating poor ripeness or overripeness. The skin may be rough, scarred, or wrinkled, suggesting damage or age.
